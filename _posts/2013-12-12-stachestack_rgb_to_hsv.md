The `stachestack_rgb_to_hsv` function has been deprecated since StacheStack 3.2

It has now moved inside the [`StacheStack_Color`](/classes/StacheStack_Color) class.

```php
<?php return StacheStack_Color::rgb_to_hsv( $rgb ); ?>
```
